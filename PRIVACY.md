# Privacy Policy for Snippet Helper

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2026

Snippet Helper ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

### 1. Personal Data Collection
**Snippet Helper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.** 
All information you create or interact with stays exclusively on your local device.

### 2. Information We Access
To provide its core functionality, the extension requires certain permissions, which are used as follows:
*   **Shortcuts & Templates:** Any text snippets or templates you create are stored 100% locally on your computer using Chrome's local storage API.
*   **Typed Text:** The extension monitors keystrokes locally to detect your "triggers" (shortcuts). This data is processed in real-time within your browser and is never logged, saved, or transmitted.
*   **ServiceNow Data:** If used on ServiceNow, the extension may read the current page context (like a ticket number or caller name) to fill your templates. This information never leaves your browser.
*   **Images:** If you use the image-paste feature, images are uploaded directly from your browser to your specific ServiceNow instance. We never see or store these images.

### 3. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. There are no third-party trackers, analytics, or advertising scripts embedded in the extension.

### 4. Data Security
Because all data is stored locally, the security of your snippets depends on the security of your personal computer and your browser profile. We recommend using Chrome's built-in profile sync if you wish to backup your settings across your own devices.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

### 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the official web store support channel.
